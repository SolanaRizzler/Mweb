About Tomy Sholby

Tomy Sholby, inspired by the iconic character Tommy Shelby from Peaky Blinders, is a symbol of cunning leadership and strategic brilliance. With a sharp mind and even sharper style, Tomy embodies the spirit of the roaring 1920s Birmingham underworld.

About Us

We are the Peaky Blinders, a formidable gang from Birmingham, England. Named for our practice of sewing razor blades into the peaks of our caps, we've risen from the back alleys of Small Heath to become a force to be reckoned with in the criminal underworld and beyond.

Under the leadership of Tomy Sholby, we've expanded our interests from illegal betting and protection rackets to legitimate businesses and political influence. Our story is one of ambition, family loyalty, and the relentless pursuit of power in a world that's changing faster than most can keep up with.
